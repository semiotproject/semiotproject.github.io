SemIoT (Semantic Tecnologies for Internet of Things) - a project of applied research and experimental developments, aiming to solve the problem of achiving interoperability of systems, applications and devices in Internet of Things.

Interoperability is a property of a product or system, whose interfaces are completely understood, to work with other products or systems, present or future, without any restricted access or implementation.
